# рҹҺ® Game Hub

A real-time multiplayer party game platform for phones and a shared screen вҖ” host on a laptop or TV, players join from their phones via QR code. No app install needed.

## Games

### рҹ‘‘ Empire
A word-guessing party game.

1. Everyone secretly submits a word (optionally within a chosen category)
2. The shuffled list of words is revealed вҖ” but not who wrote them
3. Players take turns guessing which word belongs to which person вҖ” guess correctly and you "capture" them into your empire
4. **Last empire standing wins!**

### рҹ§  Trivia
Fast-paced multiple-choice trivia with live scoring. Questions come from the Open Trivia Database.

1. The host picks a category, difficulty, number of questions, and time per question
2. Each question shows four colored answer tiles (A, B, C, D) on every phone
3. Tap the tile you think is correct вҖ” **faster correct answers earn more points**
4. After each question, see the answer bar chart and the Top 5 leaderboard
5. **After the final question, the podium reveals the top 3**

### 24
Race to 24 with four numbers and the four operations (`+`, `вҲ’`, `Г—`, `Г·`).

1. The host picks a difficulty (easy / medium / hard / any) and a round length
2. Everyone gets the same shuffled queue of puzzles on their phone
3. Tap a number, an operator, then another number to combine them вҖ” repeat until one tile is left
4. When that final tile equals **24**, you score and the next puzzle appears
5. The **faster** you solve, the **more points** you earn вҖ” stuck puzzles can be **Skipped** for a small penalty
6. **Highest total score** when the timer hits zero wins

Also includes a **Solo Practice** mode вҖ” pick a difficulty and solve unlimited puzzles on your own, with a "Give Up" reveal that shows a worked solution.

### рҹҗ„ Herd Mind
Everyone answers the same open question вҖ” score by matching the herd, and dodge the Pink Cow.

1. Each round, a question appears and everyone secretly types an answer before the timer ends
2. Everyone who gives the **most popular** answer earns a point вҖ” match the herd!
3. Give a completely **unique** answer and you're landed with the **Pink Cow** рҹҗ„
4. You **can't win while holding the Pink Cow** вҖ” pass it on by making someone else the odd one out
5. **First to the target score (without the cow) wins the herd!**

### рҹ”  Noggle
A Boggle-style word hunt on a shared, randomly shaken letter grid.

1. The host picks a board size (**4Г—4**, **5Г—5**, or **6Г—6**) and a time limit
2. When the round starts, everyone sees the **exact same shaken grid** on their phone
3. **Trace** a word by dragging across connecting letters вҖ” horizontal, vertical, or diagonal neighbours (or tap tiles one by one)
4. No reusing the same tile twice in one word, and words must be in the dictionary
5. Longer words score more (`3вҖ“4 = 1`, `5 = 2`, `6 = 3`, `7 = 5`, `8+ = 11`); the **"Qu"** tile counts as two letters
6. Minimum word length is **3** on 4Г—4 and **4** on the bigger boards
7. **When the timer hits zero, scores are revealed podium-style вҖ” highest total wins!**

Also includes a **Solo Practice** mode вҖ” pick a board size and hunt for words on a freshly shaken grid with no time limit (a count-up timer tracks your run). Tap **See all words** to reveal everything on the board and see how many you found.

### вҡҪ Soccer Head
A fast, physics-y arcade soccer showdown played on the shared screen, with each phone as a live controller.

1. Play **1v1** or **2v2** вҖ” get sorted onto Team Red or Team Blue (or add a **CPU** opponent to play solo)
2. The host sets the match length (**0:30вҖ“2:00**), then hits Start
3. Your phone becomes a controller: **left/right** to move, **jump** and **kick** on the right
4. **Double-tap** left or right for a quick **dash** (short cooldown) вҖ” dash into the ball for a power shot
5. Head, body, or boot the ball into the opponent's net to score вҖ” then fire off an **emoji reaction** during the celebration
6. **Highest score when the timer hits zero wins вҖ” a tie goes to sudden-death golden goal**

The host can **pause** mid-match from the button under the top bar (the whole game freezes and controllers are locked, then everything resumes exactly where it left off). If the ball goes untouched for **10 seconds**, the point automatically resets from a fresh centre kickoff.

### рҹҘҮ Ranking
A co-op guessing game вҖ” one player secretly ranks a list, and the group has to rebuild that order without them.

1. Each round shows a list of **5 words or phrases**
2. One player is the secret **Ranker** вҖ” they quietly put the 5 items in order from **1 to 5**
3. A different player is the **Submitter** вҖ” the whole group discusses and drags the items into the order they *think* the Ranker chose (mirrored live to the Host and everyone's phones, while the Ranker stays quiet)
4. On reveal, every item in the **matching position** scores for the **Group**; every mismatch scores for the **Game**
5. Everyone is the Ranker exactly once and the Submitter exactly once
6. **Beat the Game вҖ” the Group wins if its score ties or beats the Game's**

By default, **Custom Words** mode is on: before the first round everyone secretly submits five words of their own to build the pool (each used once). Turn it off to play from the built-in phrase bank instead.
