🔠 Noggle

Everyone gets the same shaken letter grid. Race the clock to find as many words as you can — the player with the highest score wins.

  1. Players scan the QR code to join from their phone
  2. Pick a board size (4×4, 5×5, 6×6) and a time limit, then hit Start
  3. On their phone, players trace a word by dragging across connecting letters — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal neighbours (you can also tap tiles one by one)
  4. No tile may be reused within a single word, and the word must be in the dictionary
  5. Longer words score more: 3–4 = 1, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11 points. The "Qu" tile counts as two letters
  6. Minimum length is 3 letters on 4×4, and 4 letters on the bigger boards
  7. When the timer hits zero, scores are revealed podium-style — highest total wins!
