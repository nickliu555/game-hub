⚽ Soccer Head

Big-head arcade soccer. The match plays out on this screen while each player uses their phone as a live controller.

  1. Players scan the QR code to join from their phone
  2. It's 1 v 1 — one player on Team Red, one on Team Blue (drag or click a player to switch sides)
  3. No second player? Hit 🤖 Add CPU to play against the computer
  4. Set the match length (1–4 min) and hit Kick off!
  5. On their phones: left/right to move (left side), Jump and Kick on the right
  6. Double-tap left or right for a quick dash (short cooldown)
  7. Head, body, or boot the ball into the opponent's net to score
  8. Highest score when the timer hits zero wins — a tie goes to sudden-death golden goal
