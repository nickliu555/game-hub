🥅 Sling Soccer

Turn-based top-down flick soccer. The board plays out on this screen while each player uses their phone as a slingshot controller.

  1. Players scan the QR code to join Team Red or Team Blue (drag or click a player to switch sides — up to 3 per team, at least 1 each)
  2. No opponent? Hit 🤖 Add CPU to play solo
  3. Set the goals to win (1–10), then hit Kick off!
  4. Teams take turns; players on a team rotate in join order
  5. On your turn: tap one of your 5 tokens, then pull back the slingshot — further back = more power — and release to flick it
  6. Bounce your tokens off walls, posts and each other to knock the ball into the opponent's net
  7. After each goal the board resets and the team that was scored on kicks off
  8. First team to the goal total wins!
