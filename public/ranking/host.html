🥇 Ranking

A co-operative "read the ranker's mind" game — the whole group plays together against the game.

  1. Players join by scanning the QR code — no app needed
  2. Each round, one player is the secret Ranker — they order 5 random things 1→5 however they like
  3. A different player is the Submitter; everyone else can see the 5 things and discuss
  4. Debate the Ranker's likely order out loud — but the Ranker must stay silent!
  5. The Submitter drags the group's consensus order live on this screen, then locks it in
  6. Each position that matches the Ranker scores the group; each miss scores the game
  7. Everyone is Ranker once and Submitter once — finish with a score ≥ the game's to win together!
