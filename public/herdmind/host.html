🐄 Herd Mind

Everyone answers the same open question on their phone. The goal isn't to be clever — it's to match the herd.

  1. Players join by scanning the QR code — no app needed
  2. Each round a question appears; players type a short answer before the timer ends
  3. Answers are auto-grouped into buckets — you can drag to merge, split, or rename before scoring
  4. Everyone in the most popular bucket earns a point
  5. Give a one-of-a-kind answer and you're stuck with the Pink Cow 🐄
  6. You can't win while holding the cow — first to the target score without it wins!
