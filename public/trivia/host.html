🧠 Trivia

A fast-paced multiple-choice quiz where everyone plays from their phone and the host's screen shows the questions.

  1. Players join by scanning the QR code in the lobby — no app install needed
  2. Pick a category, difficulty, question count, and time per question, then hit Start the quiz
  3. Each question shows on the host's screen; players tap one of four answers on their phone before time runs out
  4. Faster correct answers earn more points
  5. The leaderboard updates after every reveal — highest score after the final question wins!
