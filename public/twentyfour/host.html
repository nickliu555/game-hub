24 The Game

Players race to make 24 using all four numbers and any of the four operations (+ − × ÷). Each puzzle uses every number exactly once. Highest score when the timer hits zero wins.

  1. Players scan the QR code to join from their phone
  2. Pick a difficulty and a round length, then hit Start
  3. On their phone: tap a number, tap an operator, tap another number to combine them
  4. Combine until only one tile is left — if it equals 24, you score and the next puzzle appears
  5. The faster you solve, the more points you earn. Stuck? You can Skip for a small penalty
  6. Highest total score when the timer hits zero wins
