24 The Game

Players race to make 24 using all four numbers and any of the four operations (+ − × ÷). Each puzzle uses every number exactly once.

  1. Players scan the QR code to join from their phone
  2. Pick a mode, a difficulty, then hit Start
  3. Sprint: everyone solves their own stream of puzzles for a set time — the faster you solve the more points you earn, and highest total when the timer hits zero wins. Stuck? Skip for a small penalty
  4. Race: everyone gets the same problem at once and the first to solve it wins the point. First to your chosen target score wins. Each problem has a time limit; if it runs out the answer is revealed and play moves on
  5. On their phone: tap a number, tap an operator, tap another number to combine them
